What we store

A clear list of what Link-a-Link stores and what it does not.

What we store

  • Mapping data — the migration CSV your admin uploads
  • IntellinQ configurations — system names, prefixes, and base URLs

What we do not store

  • Confluence page content
  • User search queries
  • User account IDs
  • Page titles or document content
  • IP addresses, cookies, or browser fingerprints

Multi-tenant safety

Every workspace's data is completely isolated from every other workspace. Your migration index is sharded by tenant and never touches another organisation's data — even on shared Forge infrastructure.

ℹ See also

For the full privacy details, see the Privacy Policy.

Deleting your data

How admins permanently delete Link-a-Link data.

Privacy & Data — Danger Zone with deletion controls

Steps

  1. Open Link-a-Link admin → Privacy & Data
  2. Expand the Danger Zone
  3. Click "Delete All Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Deletion is immediate and irrecoverable. All mapping data, IntellinQ configurations, and search history are permanently removed.

⚠ Cannot be undone

This permanently removes all data. Export anything you need first. The deletion is audit-logged with timestamp and actor ID.
